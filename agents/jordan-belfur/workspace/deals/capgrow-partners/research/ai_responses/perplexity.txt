**OpenCorporates is an open database of companies that aggregates and unifies company data from official registers worldwide, including over 50 US state-level registries (with Illinois forthcoming), enabling unified searches across jurisdictions without manual checks per state.[1]**

### What They Do
OpenCorporates collects company data from disparate sources, standardizes it according to a unified schema, and makes it searchable in one place, addressing the lack of a centralized US company database where registrations occur at state, District of Columbia, and territory levels with inconsistent data structures and access methods.[1]

### Who They Serve
They serve researchers, businesses, and users needing quick company searches across the US (or 140+ global jurisdictions), as well as those requiring bulk data or API access for automated workflows, data analytics, or large-scale company population analysis.[1]
