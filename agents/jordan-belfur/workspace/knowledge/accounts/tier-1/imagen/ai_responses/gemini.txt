**What is Imagen?**

Imagen is a cloud-native video management platform. They provide solutions for organizations to manage, store, distribute, and monetize their video content.  Think of them as a centralized hub for all things video, from ingesting raw footage to delivering it to end-users, partners, or broadcast channels.

**What do they do?**

Imagen's core functionalities include:

*   **Video Ingestion & Storage:**  Securely store and manage large video libraries in the cloud.
*   **Video Management:** Organize, tag, and search video assets efficiently with metadata.
*   **Video Editing & Collaboration:** Tools for basic editing, clipping, and collaborative workflows.
*   **Video Distribution:**  Deliver video content to websites, apps, social media, and other platforms.
*   **Video Monetization:**  Tools to generate revenue through licensing, subscriptions, or advertising.
*   **AI-Powered Capabilities:** Leveraging AI for tasks like automated metadata tagging, object recognition, and speech-to-text transcription.
