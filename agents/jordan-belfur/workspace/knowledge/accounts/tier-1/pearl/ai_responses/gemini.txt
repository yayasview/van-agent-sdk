**What is Pearl?**

Pearl is a technology company that develops and provides artificial intelligence (AI) solutions for the dental industry. Their AI software is designed to help dentists improve accuracy, efficiency, and consistency in diagnosing and treating patients.

**What do they do?**

Pearl's AI software performs several key functions:

*   **Radiographic Analysis:** Analyzes dental X-rays to automatically detect a wide range of conditions, including cavities (caries), bone loss, root issues, and other anomalies.
*   **Treatment Planning Support:** Helps dentists develop comprehensive and accurate treatment plans by providing insights and suggestions based on the AI's analysis.
*   **Patient Education:** Supports dentists in explaining conditions and treatment options to patients using visual aids and AI-powered insights.
*   **Quality Assurance:** Assists in ensuring consistency and accuracy in diagnosis and treatment across a dental practice or organization.
*   **Practice Management Optimization:** Supports processes such as insurance pre-authorization.
