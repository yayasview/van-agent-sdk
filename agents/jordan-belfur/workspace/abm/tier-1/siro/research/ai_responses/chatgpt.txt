Siro is a telecommunications company providing high-speed broadband internet services primarily in Ireland. They utilize a fiber-to-the-building (FTTB) network, delivering gigabit speeds to residential and business customers by leveraging existing electrical infrastructure. Their focus on innovative technology and reliable service positions them as a key player in the broadband market. Siro is known for its commitment to enhancing connectivity and supporting digital transformation across its service regions. Customer feedback often highlights their consistent performance and high-speed delivery.
