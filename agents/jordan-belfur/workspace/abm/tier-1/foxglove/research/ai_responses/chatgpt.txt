Foxglove is a company specializing in robotics software tools that aim to improve the development, visualization, and debugging of robot applications. Serving robotics engineers and developers, they provide features such as real-time telemetry visualization, interactive data analysis, and integration with robotic frameworks. Foxglove distinguishes itself through its open-source offerings, fostering community engagement and rapid innovation. Known for enhancing productivity in the robotics development cycle, customers have praised their intuitive interfaces and robust integrations that streamline complex workflows.
