Okay, let's break down EventsAir:

**What is EventsAir?**

EventsAir is a comprehensive, cloud-based event management platform.  Think of it as an all-in-one software solution for managing events of all sizes and complexities, from small meetings to large-scale conferences and exhibitions.

**What do they do?**

EventsAir provides a wide range of features and tools to streamline the entire event lifecycle.  This includes:

*   **Event Planning & Management:**  Tools for creating event websites, managing speakers and sponsors, tracking budgets, and creating event timelines.
*   **Registration & Ticketing:**  Customizable registration forms, online payment processing, attendee management, and badge printing.
*   **Venue & Accommodation Management:**  Tools for managing venue contracts, room blocks, and attendee accommodation.
*   **Communication & Marketing:**  Email marketing, social media integration, push notifications, and event apps to keep attendees informed.
*   **Onsite Management:**  Tools for check-in, session tracking, lead retrieval, and exhibitor management.
*   **Reporting & Analytics:**  Detailed reporting on event attendance, registration trends, financial performance, and attendee engagement.
*   **Virtual and Hybrid Event Support:** Features and integrations to manage online events, hybrid events, and virtual components of in-person events.
*   **Mobile Event App:** A customizable mobile app for attendees to access event information, network, and engage with content.

*   **Event Planners & Agencies:** Professional event management companies that organize events for clients.
*   **Associations & Non-Profits:** Organizations that host conferences, meetings, and fundraising events.
*   **Corporations:** Businesses that organize internal meetings, conferences, trade shows, and product launches.
*   **Government & Education:** Public sector organizations and educational institutions that host events.
*   **Venues:** Venue management companies that use EventsAir to manage and promote their spaces

Essentially, anyone who plans and manages events can benefit from using EventsAir.  They cater to both large and small organizations.
