**EventsAir is a comprehensive event management software platform** that helps event professionals plan, execute, and analyze in-person, virtual, and hybrid events[1][3].

## What They Do

EventsAir provides an **all-in-one platform** that covers the entire event lifecycle[1]. Pre-event capabilities include custom-branded event websites, registration management, speaker and abstract management, budget control, and logistics planning[3]. During the event, the platform facilitates seamless check-in experiences, mobile attendee apps, real-time sponsor dashboards, and hybrid content streaming[3]. Post-event, EventsAir offers 150+ reports for tracking engagement and ROI, along with content repurposing tools[3].

## Who They Serve

The platform is **designed by event planners for event planners**[2]. EventsAir serves professional conference organizers, corporate event planners, industry associations, government conferences, and university faculties[2]. They have powered over 400,000 events worldwide, generating 18 million registrations and 2.5 million online attendees[4]. Notable clients include major global events such as the Olympics, G20, APEC, Rugby World Cup, and Commonwealth Games[2].

## What Makes Them Different

EventsAir has been **innovating for over 30 years** since its founding in 1990[4]. The platform is built on Microsoft Azure's secure, scalable cloud infrastructure with PCI compliance and GDPR toolkit support[2]. A distinguishing achievement is **OnAIR**, a rapid innovation developed in May 2020 that enabled event planners to continue delivering experiences when in-person meetings became impossible, adding remote attendance capabilities to live events[4].
