## Account Progression Tracking

### Status Framework
Accounts progress through a defined engagement lifecycle that ensures structured deal development and forward momentum:

**Status Options:**
- **Target:** Initial ABM outreach not yet initiated
- **Engaged:** First contact made, initial response received, or early-stage conversations underway
- **Meeting:** Formal discovery call or meeting scheduled/completed
- **Opportunity:** Deal identified with specific project scope and budget discussion initiated
- **Closed:** Deal won or account removed from pipeline

### Movement Rules
- **Weekly Review:** Every account must show forward motion week-over-week
- **Move or Replace:** Accounts stalled at any stage for 2+ weeks are automatically replaced with next-priority targets
- **Minimum Engagement:** Tier 1 accounts require at least one touch per week; Tier 2 tracked at segment level with bi-weekly reviews

### Tier 1 Account Tracking
- **Primary Owner:** Assigned AE with individual account plan
- **Custom ABM Properties in HubSpot:** Contact history, engagement level, decision maker mapping, competitive intel, custom deal stage
- **Individual Views:** Separate HubSpot view per Tier 1 account with activity timeline and next steps
- **Cadence:** Weekly status updates, monthly business reviews with stakeholders

### Tier 2 Account Tracking
- **Segment Owner:** ABM marketer owns segment-level engagement strategy
- **Aggregated Pipeline View:** All 34 accounts tracked at segment level with priority ranking
- **Custom Fields:** Segment cluster, propensity score, engagement trigger status
- **Cadence:** Bi-weekly segment reviews; accounts escalated to Tier 1 when opportunity signals emerge

### Pipeline Target
- **Q1 2026 Goal:** $1.5M qualified ABM pipeline
- **Distribution:** Tier 1 accounts = 70% pipeline ($1.05M); Tier 2 = 30% pipeline ($450K)
- **Win Rate Assumption:** 25% conversion (Tier 1), 12% conversion (Tier 2)
- **Revenue Target:** $375K Q1 ARR impact from ABM accounts (assuming 9-month sales cycle)

### Reporting & Escalation
- **Weekly:** Pipeline velocity report (accounts moving between stages)
- **Monthly:** Revenue impact analysis, account health scorecard, competitive win/loss analysis
- **Quarterly:** ABM performance review vs. pipeline target, account replacement recommendations
