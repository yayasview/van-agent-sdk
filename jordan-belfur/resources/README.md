This folder contains reference materials that inform and fuel the BDR agent's outputs. The more context you provide here, the better the agent performs.

## What to Add Here

### Ideal Client Profile (ICP)
- Target company characteristics (size, industry, tech stack, revenue)
- Buyer personas and titles
- Common pain points by segment
- Disqualification criteria

### VAN Service Catalog
- Service offerings with descriptions
- Capabilities by agency within VAN
- Case studies and results
- Differentiators vs. competitors

### Pricing & Packaging
- Rate cards or pricing tiers
- Common engagement models (retainer, project, hybrid)
- Discount policies or approval thresholds

### Sales Collateral
- One-pagers, pitch decks
- Case studies
- Testimonials and social proof
- Competitive battle cards

### Process Documentation
- Sales process stages (mapped to HubSpot pipeline)
- Qualification frameworks (BANT, MEDDIC, etc.)
- Handoff procedures
- Escalation paths

### Brand & Tone
- Brand voice guidelines
- Email signature format
- Communication do's and don'ts

## File Formats

Any format works: `.md`, `.docx`, `.pdf`, `.txt`, `.html`, `.pptx`

The BDR agent will read and reference these materials when generating outputs.
